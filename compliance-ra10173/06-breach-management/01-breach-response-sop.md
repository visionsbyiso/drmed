1. Detect and Contain
- Immediately isolate affected system/process.

2. Escalate
- Notify DPO and management immediately.

3. Assess
- Determine data affected, number of data subjects, risk, and root cause.

4. Notify
- If notifiable, submit NPC breach notice within 72 hours from knowledge.
- Notify affected data subjects if required.

5. Remediate
- Apply technical/operational fixes.

6. Document
- Complete incident report, timeline, actions, and preventive controls.

7. Review
- Conduct post-incident review and update controls.
