# Annual Internal Privacy Audit Checklist

- [ ] DPO details current and documented.
- [ ] NPCRS records current.
- [ ] Privacy notice and terms updated/versioned.
- [ ] Consent logging active and sample-verified.
- [ ] Access control review completed.
- [ ] Vendor/DPA register updated.
- [ ] Retention disposal actions documented.
- [ ] DSR requests handled within SLA.
- [ ] Breach drill conducted.
- [ ] ASIR/required NPC submissions completed.
