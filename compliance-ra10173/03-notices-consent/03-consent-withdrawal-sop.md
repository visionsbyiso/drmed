1. Receive withdrawal request via official channel (email/hotline/in-person form).
2. Verify requester identity.
3. Log request in DSR register within 24 hours.
4. Determine if processing can stop immediately or must continue due to legal/medical obligations.
5. Confirm action to requester in writing.
6. Update affected systems/workflows.
7. Close request with date, action taken, and approver.

Target turnaround: within 15 calendar days unless legally justified.
