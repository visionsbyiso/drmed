# Privacy Notice Checklist

- [ ] Identity/contact details of controller are present.
- [ ] Data categories are described.
- [ ] Purposes of processing are listed.
- [ ] Lawful bases are stated.
- [ ] Data sharing/recipients are described.
- [ ] Retention period or criteria is stated.
- [ ] Data subject rights are listed.
- [ ] Complaint/contact channel is provided.
- [ ] Last updated date is shown.
- [ ] Version identifier is shown (example: 2026-02-24).
