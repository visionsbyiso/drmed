# Staff Security Policy (Template)

Last updated: March 10, 2026

This policy applies to all staff with access to patient data, including the portal, Google Sheets, and Google Drive.

## 1) Acceptable use

- Access is for official clinic 업무 only.
- Do not share patient data outside authorized channels.
- Do not use personal email, storage, or messaging apps to transmit patient data.

## 2) Authentication

- Use your assigned staff user + password only.
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters and not reused.
- MFA is required if enabled for your account.
- Do not share passwords or MFA codes.

## 3) Devices and storage

- Use clinic-approved devices only.
- Lock your device when unattended.
- Do not store patient files on personal devices.

## 4) Data handling

- Access only the minimum data required for your task.
- Confirm patient identity before release.
- If a result is pending, do not release.

## 5) Incident reporting

- Report suspected incidents immediately to the DPO/IT.
- Do not attempt to delete or hide evidence.

## 6) Access termination

- Access is revoked upon resignation or role change.
- Return all clinic devices and credentials.
